How It Works

Versara is a peer-to-peer mobile renting market for household items. List anything from computers, cameras, and costumes to other users in the local area that have temporary needs.

Our process is made simple, frictionless and safe for all users.

We guarantee...

  • safety and security: users will need background checks and payment encryption.
  • frictionless trades: trade at designated Versara points at designated times in public locations
  • insurance for every item you list
  • easy UI: explore our map and price tags with ease
